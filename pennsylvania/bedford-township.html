HVAC FAQ — Bedford, Pennsylvania

How much does a furnace replacement cost in Bedford, PA?
Furnace replacement in Bedford typically runs $3657 to $9405 for a natural gas system, including the unit, venting, thermostat and labor. High-efficiency (96%+ AFUE) models cost more upfront but save significantly on Pennsylvania gas bills — often paying back in 3-5 years. The final price depends on home size, BTU requirements, and whether venting modifications are needed. A licensed Bedford HVAC contractor will perform a load calculation to properly size your system.
Is a heat pump a good option in Pennsylvania?
Yes — modern cold-climate heat pumps are now rated down to -13°F and work efficiently in Bedford's winters. A heat pump provides both heating and cooling, potentially replacing both your AC and furnace. Efficiency is measured in HSPF2 for heating — look for 9+ for PA climates. Installation costs $5016-$13063 for a full system, but Inflation Reduction Act tax credits (up to $2,000) and PA utility rebates can significantly offset costs. Our Bedford contractors can advise on current incentives.
When should I replace vs. repair my AC in Bedford, PA?
General rule: if your AC is over 12 years old and repair cost exceeds $1,000 (e.g., compressor failure), replacement is usually the better investment at $4389-$10973. If the system is under 10 years old and the repair is under $500-600, repair makes sense. The '5,000 rule': multiply the system's age by the repair cost — if over $5,000, replace. Also consider: R-22 refrigerant (being phased out, very expensive to recharge), rising energy bills, and uneven cooling. A licensed Bedford HVAC tech can advise after diagnosis.
How long does AC installation take in Bedford?
A standard central AC or heat pump installation in Bedford — replacing an existing system — is typically completed in one day. New installations requiring ductwork additions may take 2-3 days. Your licensed Bedford HVAC contractor will pull the required permits before work begins (same-day permits are common in most PA municipalities). After installation, the system is pressure-tested, charged with refrigerant, and tested for proper operation before the crew leaves.
How much does a new AC system cost in Bedford, PA?
A new central air conditioning system in Bedford typically costs between $4389 and $10973, including the outdoor condenser unit, air handler or coil, refrigerant line set, thermostat, and labor. Price varies based on home size, efficiency rating (SEER2), and whether ductwork modifications are needed. Heat pump systems range from $5016 to $13063. Get a free written estimate from a licensed Bedford HVAC contractor before committing.
How long does an HVAC system last in Pennsylvania?
In Bedford's climate — hot, humid summers and cold winters — a well-maintained central AC system typically lasts 15-20 years. Gas furnaces last 20-25 years. Heat pumps last 15-20 years. Regular annual maintenance (tune-ups at $104-$397) significantly extends equipment life and efficiency. Signs it's time to replace: system is 15+ years old, frequent repairs, R-22 refrigerant (no longer produced), or rising energy bills despite maintenance.
